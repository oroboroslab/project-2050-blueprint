Key Judgments

KJ-1

Pure capitalism, pure state control, pure open source, and pure proprietary have each failed to deliver sustained innovation, equitable distribution, or long-term civilizational stability.

KJ-2

A hybrid framework — retaining what each paradigm produces functionally, rejecting what it produces destructively — is both viable and necessary.

KJ-3

Autonomous robotic construction (TRITON-class) collapses structural build times from months to days. This single capability makes the 25-year timeline achievable.

KJ-4

Global public opinion is already shifting toward U.S.–China collaboration as necessary and inevitable. Polling confirms the trajectory.

KJ-5

The post-scarcity economic model is achievable within the 25-year window — if construction phase begins no later than 2028.
